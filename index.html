Healthcare Questions

Get answers to common questions about our healthcare services

How does Plexus Care's virtual consultation work?
+
Our virtual consultations connect you with board-certified doctors through secure video calls. Simply book an appointment, and our system will guide you through the process. You can discuss your health concerns, get prescriptions, and receive medical advice from the comfort of your home. All consultations are HIPAA-compliant and confidential.
Can I get prescriptions through Plexus Care?
+
Yes, our licensed physicians can prescribe medications when appropriate during your virtual visit. We can send prescriptions directly to your preferred pharmacy for easy pickup. However, certain controlled substances may require an in-person visit as per federal regulations.
Is my health information secure with Plexus Care?
+
Absolutely. We take your privacy and security seriously. Our platform uses bank-level encryption and complies with all HIPAA regulations. Your health records are stored securely and only shared with healthcare providers you authorize. You have full control over your data.
How do you address postpartum depression and anxiety?
+
We provide evidence-based treatment including cognitive behavioral therapy, support groups, and psychiatric consultations when needed. Our screening tools help identify symptoms early, and we offer 24/7 crisis support for urgent situations.
Is emotional support available outside of regular appointments?
+
Absolutely! We offer 24/7 emotional support through our peer counselor chat system, online support communities, and crisis hotline. Our mobile app provides guided meditations, mood tracking, and instant access to resources.
What are your pricing options and insurance coverage?
+
We accept most major insurance plans and offer sliding scale fees based on income. Many services are covered under maternal health benefits, and we provide assistance with insurance navigation and prior authorization processes.
What insurance plans do you accept?
+
We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. We also offer affordable self-pay options for those without insurance. Our team can help verify your coverage and explain any out-of-pocket costs before your visit. Check our insurance page for the most up-to-date list of accepted providers.
